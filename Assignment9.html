Columbia Missourian: CollegeTown

Overview

For my Multimedia Planning & Design class, I was placed in a group with 4 other students to code a website using HTML, CSS and Javascript. Currently, our assignment is to create a microsite that branches off the local newspaper, the Columbia Missourian, for their "CollegeTown" section.

Target Audience

Our target audience for the CollegeTown section includes incoming college freshmen or transfers ages 17-22 who will be students at any of the three colleges in Columbia. This section can also appeal to nontraditional students at the colleges or masters/Ph.D. students who are new to Columbia, so the age range may vary.

Solutions and Goals

For our design direction, our website will be featured on the Missourian website but will retain its own independent style aspects. We want this website to be an easy to navigate, visually appealing and helpful website for upcoming students, transfers and family members that make their way to Columbia. We also want to be sure that our website is not isolated to just Mizzou students and family members but also caters to students at Columbia College and Stephens University. One of the biggest impetus’ for this project was to incentivize advertisers to advertise digitally, so we will be sure to put a great deal of energy into maximizing the opportunities for digital advertisers.
